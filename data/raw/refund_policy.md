# Refund Policy

Customers may request refunds under the following conditions:

1. The request is made within 30 days of purchase.
2. The product has not been significantly used.
3. Subscription services can be prorated.

Refund processing steps:

1. Verify the purchase ID.
2. Confirm the request falls within the refund window.
3. Issue refund through the billing portal.
4. Notify the customer via email.
