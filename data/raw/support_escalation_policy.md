# Support Escalation Policy

Certain issues require escalation to Tier 2 or Tier 3 engineering teams.

## Escalate immediately if:

- The issue affects more than 50 users
- Production systems are down
- Security vulnerabilities are suspected

## Tier 2 escalation examples

- Persistent authentication failures
- MFA reset issues not resolved by standard procedure
- Database connection errors

## Tier 3 escalation examples

- Data corruption
- Infrastructure outages
